# 📌 About the Project

Electricity is a vital driver of economic growth, industrial productivity, and quality of life. Understanding electricity consumption trends helps policymakers, businesses, and researchers make informed decisions regarding energy planning and infrastructure development.

This project analyzes **state-wise electricity consumption across India from January 2019 to December 2020**, a period that includes the **COVID-19 pandemic and nationwide lockdown**.

By examining consumption patterns before, during, and after the lockdown, the project highlights how economic disruptions and regional differences influence electricity demand.

---

# ❗ Problem Statement

India’s electricity consumption patterns vary widely across regions due to differences in:

- Industrial activity  
- Population density  
- Climate conditions  
- Economic development  

The **COVID-19 lockdown in 2020** significantly disrupted normal electricity usage patterns. Understanding these changes helps identify how different regions responded to the economic slowdown and how quickly electricity demand recovered.

---

# 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **state-wise electricity consumption data across India**.

### Key Features

- **Time Period:** January 2019 – December 2020  
- **Granularity:** Monthly electricity consumption  
- **Coverage:** Major Indian states  
- **Attributes Include:**
  - State Name
  - Region
  - Month & Year
  - Electricity Consumption

This dataset allows the analysis of both **temporal trends and regional electricity demand variations**.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

- Analyze **monthly electricity consumption trends across India**
- Identify the **impact of COVID-19 lockdown on electricity demand**
- Compare **electricity usage patterns across different regions**
- Study the **recovery of electricity demand after the lockdown**
- Present insights through **data visualization**

---

# 📊 Key Analytical Scenarios

## Scenario 1 – Change in Overall Consumption Trends

Electricity consumption patterns from **2019 to 2020** show notable changes influenced by seasonal demand, economic fluctuations, and the COVID-19 lockdown.

Key aspects analyzed:

- Monthly consumption patterns
- Seasonal electricity demand
- Impact of lockdown on electricity usage
- Comparison of consumption between 2019 and 2020

---

## Scenario 2 – Regional Variations in Demand

Electricity consumption differs across Indian regions due to factors such as climate, industrial activity, and population density.

The project compares electricity demand across:

- Northern Region  
- Southern Region  
- Eastern Region  
- Western Region  
- Northeastern Region  

This helps identify regions with **higher or lower electricity demand**.

---

## Scenario 3 – Recovery After Lockdown

After the nationwide lockdown (March–June 2020), electricity demand gradually started to recover.

This analysis focuses on:

- Comparing **pre-lockdown and post-lockdown consumption**
- Identifying **states with faster recovery**
- Highlighting regions where electricity demand remained below previous levels
